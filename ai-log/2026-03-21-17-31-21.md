## Response Summary

Explained that Sourcetree's OAuth App integration does not request the `workflow` scope and this cannot be changed by the user — it is fixed by the app developer. Provided three options: (1) push from terminal directly, (2) use the PAT from .env as a temporary remote URL override for a one-time push, (3) long-term fix of switching Sourcetree from OAuth to PAT authentication by removing the OAuth account in Sourcetree Preferences and re-adding with the existing PAT after adding `workflow` scope to it in GitHub settings.
